Abstract

I am a Postdoc at ETH Zurich under the guidance of Dennis Hofheinz. Previously, I was a PhD student at École Normale Supérieure in Paris under the supervision of Brice Minaud and Michel Abdalla. I wrote my master and bachelor thesis under the supervision of Geoffroy Couteau.
The topic of my PhD was Searchable Encryption with a focus on memory-efficient constructions. My thesis can be found here. I am interested in cryptography at large, particularly in efficient constructions of encryption or signature variants.
