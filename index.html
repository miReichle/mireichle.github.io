Abstract

I am a Postdoc at ETH Zurich under the guidance of Dennis Hofheinz. Previously, I was a PhD student at École Normale Supérieure in Paris under the supervision of Brice Minaud and Michel Abdalla. I wrote my master and bachelor thesis under the supervision of Geoffroy Couteau.
The topic of my PhD was Searchable Encryption with a focus on memory-efficient constructions. My thesis can be found here. My current research focuses on advanced forms of authentication (e.g., blind signatures and threshold signatures). My other interests include variants of encryption and zero-knowledge.
