about

I am a PhD student at École Normale Supérieure de Paris and Inria, under the supervision of Brice Minaud. I wrote my master (resp. bachelor) thesis under the supervision of Geoffroy Couteau at IRIF (resp. Karlsruhe Institute of Technology) on the topic of range proofs (resp. anonymous credentials). The main topic of my PhD is Searchable Encryption in the area of memory efficiency. I am also interested in other areas of cryptography, such as zero-knowledge.
